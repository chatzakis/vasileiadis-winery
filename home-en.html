A few words about Vasiliadis Wine

Starting from a small vineyard in the Scholari of Thessaloniki, passing by the ancestral wine press, it reaches the your glass the 100% organic Vasiliadis Dry Wine. Vasiliadis Kyriakos has been exploring art since 2018 of winemaking and creates unique new flavors with respect for tradition. Putting personal effort and care at every stage of the process guarantees an experience different from the one you usually find in store shelves. Full and clean flavors that accompany the family table and bring us close to the values of our ancestors.

From the vineyard to your table

The vineyard

Wine begins its journey from the vine. When the grapes are ripe enough they are carefully picked with the hand. Only the best are kept for the next stage and the stalks are carefully removed.

The wine press

The next stage is the winepress. There the grapes are pressed in the traditional way to give their sweet must.

The Barrel

The must is then put into special barrels to ferment and turn into wine.

The glass

After months of maturation the wine is packaged and arrives at your table without additional additives and conservatively to accompany the moments that really count!
